    Jalpa Dance Academy was originally established in 2011 in Chicago southwest suburbs for all the dancers to find the best in them no matter what their age and experience is. The academy has moved to Georgia in 2016 with same vision. We believe in providing quality dance education to our students to become a perfect dancer. We make every effort to offer a positive environment for dancers to grow and have a great learning experience.
